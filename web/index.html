About

❝ We understand the frustration of modern research. Traditional library systems force you to guess the exact keywords an author used, leading to hours of wasted time sifting through irrelevant results. This "semantic gap" limits productivity and research quality. ❞

❝ Our mission is to bridge that gap. KnowWhere transforms the discovery process by using Hybrid Retrieval-Augmented Generation. ❞

❝ We empower students and researchers to find conceptually relevant materials instantly, improving the quality of academic output for the entire institution. We don't just find resources; we find knowledge. ❞
